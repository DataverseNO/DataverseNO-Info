# Why use DataverseNO?

## Meet funder and publisher requirements

Many research funders — including the Research Council of Norway and EU Horizon projects — require that research data be made openly available. Publishing your data in DataverseNO satisfies these requirements.

## Get a DOI for your dataset

Every published dataset receives a persistent DOI, making your data citable and traceable.

## Increase the impact of your research

Openly shared data is more discoverable, reusable, and citable, increasing the overall impact of your research.

## Free and supported

DataverseNO is free to use. Curation support is provided by trained curators who review your dataset before publication.

## FAIR data

DataverseNO is designed to help you make your data Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable (FAIR).
