# Who can use DataverseNO?

DataverseNO is available to researchers affiliated with Norwegian higher education and research institutions.

## Eligible depositors

- Researchers at Norwegian universities and university colleges
- Researchers at Norwegian research institutes
- PhD candidates affiliated with a Norwegian institution

## What about restricted data?

DataverseNO is designed for openly shared research data. Data requiring protection (personal data, sensitive data, classified data) **cannot** be deposited.

If your data requires restricted access, contact your institution's data management office for guidance on suitable alternatives.

## International collaborators

If you are an international collaborator on a Norwegian research project, contact your Norwegian project lead — they can deposit on the project's behalf.
