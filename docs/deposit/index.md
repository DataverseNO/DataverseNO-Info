# Deposit guidelines

These guidelines walk you through the process of depositing a dataset in DataverseNO, from preparing your data to citing it after publication.

!!! tip "New to DataverseNO?"
    Start with [What is DataverseNO?](../about/what-is-dataverseno.md) to understand the repository before depositing.

## Steps

1. [Prepare your data](prepare.md) — organise files, choose formats, write documentation
2. [Deposit and publish](deposit-and-publish.md) — upload your data and submit for curation
3. [Refer to your data](refer.md) — cite your dataset in publications

## What happens after you submit?

After submission, a DataverseNO curator will review your dataset. Curation typically takes a few business days. The curator may contact you with questions or suggestions before the dataset is published.
