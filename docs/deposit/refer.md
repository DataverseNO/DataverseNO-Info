# Refer to your data

Once your dataset is published, it has a permanent DOI you can use in citations.

## Citing your dataset

Use the citation displayed on your dataset's landing page in DataverseNO. A standard format is:

> Author(s) (Year). *Title of dataset*. DataverseNO. https://doi.org/10.xxxx/xxxxx

## Linking from your publication

Include the DOI in your publication's data availability statement, for example:

> The data underlying this study are available in DataverseNO at https://doi.org/10.xxxx/xxxxx

## Dataset versioning

If you update your dataset after publication, DataverseNO creates a new version while preserving the previous one. Both versions remain accessible and citable.
