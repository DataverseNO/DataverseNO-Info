For questions about depositing data or using DataverseNO, contact us at:

**Email:** [dataverse@uit.no](mailto:dataverse@uit.no)

## Repository website

To deposit or browse datasets, visit [dataverse.no](https://dataverse.no).

## Institutional support

Many Norwegian institutions have local research data support staff who can assist with data management planning and depositing. Contact your institution's library or research support office.
