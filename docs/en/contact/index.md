# Contact

## Support

For questions about depositing data, curation, or collection management, contact your **local support service** at your institution. A list of contacts per institution is available on the [support page](https://site.uit.no/dataverseno/contact/).

## General inquiries

For general questions about DataverseNO — including questions about becoming a partner institution — contact the DataverseNO repository management:

**Email:** [support@dataverse.no](mailto:support@dataverse.no)

## Technical issues

If you experience technical issues with the DataverseNO repository ([dataverse.no](https://dataverse.no/)), contact [support@dataverse.no](mailto:support@dataverse.no) and describe the issue in as much detail as possible, including any error messages and the URL of the page where the issue occurred.

## Mailing list

Stay updated on news and changes to DataverseNO by subscribing to our mailing list. Contact [support@dataverse.no](mailto:support@dataverse.no) to subscribe.
