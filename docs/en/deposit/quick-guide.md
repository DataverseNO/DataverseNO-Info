# Quick Guide to Depositing in DataverseNO

This guide gives a quick overview of the steps to deposit a dataset in DataverseNO. Follow the links for detailed instructions.

## Before you start

- [ ] Confirm your data can be openly shared (no personal data, sensitive information, etc.)
- [ ] Check that you hold the necessary rights and permissions to share the data
- [ ] Contact your [local support](https://site.uit.no/dataverseno/contact/) if you are unsure

## Step 1: Prepare your data

- [ ] Organise your files and use consistent, descriptive file names
- [ ] Convert to [preferred file formats](prepare-your-data.md#preferred-file-formats) where possible
- [ ] Write a README file describing your dataset
- [ ] Fill in metadata accurately and in English (or standard scientific language)

[:octicons-arrow-right-24: Full instructions: Prepare your data](prepare-your-data.md)

## Step 2: Deposit and publish

- [ ] Log in to [DataverseNO](https://dataverse.no/loginpage.xhtml?redirectPage=%2Fdataverse.xhtml) with your institutional credentials
- [ ] Navigate to your institution's collection and create a new dataset
- [ ] Upload your files and fill in the metadata form
- [ ] Submit the dataset for curation

[:octicons-arrow-right-24: Full instructions: Deposit your data](deposit-your-data.md)

## Step 3: Curation

- [ ] A curator from your institution will review your dataset
- [ ] Respond to any feedback and make requested changes
- [ ] The curator will publish your dataset when it meets the requirements

## Step 4: After publication

- [ ] Use the DOI to cite and refer to your dataset in publications
- [ ] Share the dataset link with collaborators and reviewers

[:octicons-arrow-right-24: Refer to your data](refer-to-your-data.md) | [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Update your data](modify-your-data.md)
