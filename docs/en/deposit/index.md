# Deposit Guidelines

On this page, we describe how research data must be prepared (1) before they can be published in DataverseNO, how the deposit process (2) works, and how you can refer (3) to your own or others' data.

!!! warning "Data requiring protection"
    DataverseNO only accepts data that can be openly shared. If your data requires protection — including personal data, health data, Indigenous data, copyrighted materials, commercially sensitive data, sensitive biodiversity data, or data with geopolitical or security concerns — please **contact your [local support](https://site.uit.no/dataverseno/contact/) before preparing your dataset**.

    You may only share data for which you hold the necessary rights and permissions.
