# Who can use DataverseNO?

DataverseNO offers support for different use cases, depending on who you are.

## Sharing data in DataverseNO

Researchers affiliated with a Norwegian research institution can use DataverseNO for free to archive and share their research data.

### Researchers from partner institutions

If you are affiliated with a DataverseNO partner institution, there are currently no limitations to the amount of data you can archive and share in the repository (but note the size limitations on individual datasets).

All partner institutions have well-established research support units that communicate closely with researchers during the depositing and curation processes. You may contact your local support by depositing a dataset for review in your institutional collection or by reaching out on the [support page](https://site.uit.no/dataverseno/contact/).

### Researchers from Norwegian non-partner institutions

If you are affiliated with a Norwegian research institution that is not a DataverseNO partner, you can receive up to 10 GB storage space for free. Your data will be curated by support staff at UiT. If your dataset exceeds 10 GB, please contact [support@dataverse.no](mailto:support@dataverse.no).

### Researchers from institutions outside Norway

If you are not affiliated with a Norwegian research institution, you may still be able to deposit datasets in DataverseNO if you collaborate with a researcher from a Norwegian institution who is also one of the authors of the dataset.

More information about creating a user account is available in the [deposit guidelines](../deposit/index.md).

## Reusing data in DataverseNO

Datasets published in DataverseNO are searchable in all major discovery services for research data. You can also search and browse all DataverseNO collections directly in the [repository](https://dataverse.no/).

Before reusing data, review the dataset-specific terms of reuse and the accompanying data license. Regardless of the license type, good scientific practice expects proper credit via citation. Please use the data citation provided on the dataset page.

## Becoming a partner institution

Norwegian research institutions can join the DataverseNO consortium to offer their researchers unlimited access to a trustworthy data repository. To join, the institution must sign a partner agreement and comply with the [DataverseNO Organizational Agreement](https://site.uit.no/dataverseno/organizational-agreement/).

The DataverseNO consortium currently includes 17 partner institutions and is open to new partners.

!!! info "Size limitations on individual datasets"
    DataverseNO currently supports small and medium-sized datasets. For datasets exceeding 5 TB in total size, we recommend using another repository. We also recommend limiting datasets to approximately 300 files to ensure smooth handling of file-level DOIs.

For more information about becoming a partner institution, contact [support@dataverse.no](mailto:support@dataverse.no).
